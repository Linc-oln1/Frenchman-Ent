ABOUT US

FRANCHMAN ENTERPRISE

Franchman Enterprise, Inc. is a private car sales company that was founded in 1993.The company is known
for its unique business model, which involves buying and reconditioning used cars and then selling them to
customers at fixed prices. Franchman Enterprise has over 5 locations across Ghana and is one of the largest
used car retailers in the country. The company offers a wide range of vehicles, including sedans, SUVs, trucks,
and sports cars, from a variety of manufacturers.

One of the key advantages of buying a car from Franchman Enterprise is the company's rigorous inspection and reconditioning
process. Every Franchman Enterprise vehicle undergoes a 125+ point inspection, which includes checking the engine,transmission,
brakes,and other key components. If any issues are found, Franchman Enterprise will fix them before the vehicle is put up for sale.
Franchman Enterprise also offers a range of financing options, including loans and leases, and has a no-haggle pricing policy, which
means that the price you see is the price you pay. The company also offers a seven-day return policy, which allows customers to return
their vehicle for any reason within the first seven days of ownership.

Overall, Franchman Enterprise is a private car sales company that offers a unique and customer-friendly approach to buying
used cars. Its rigorous inspection process, no-haggle pricing policy, and seven-day return policy make it a popular
choice for customers looking for a hassle-free car buying experience.
